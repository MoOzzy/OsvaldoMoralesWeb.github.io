Hello!
Welcome to my page

I am a graduate of UC San Diego with
a Bachelor of Science degree in Applied Mathematics. I aspire to become a Data Analyst because I believe data is essential in almost every aspect of the professional world, whether it may- be in business or research institutions. With data, we can organize vast amounts of information and solve problems by providing clear visualizations.

In December of 2004, I moved to the United States of America without having any understanding of the English language. In my first years at school, I struggled with learning due to the language barrier, except for learning the subject of mathematics. In mathematics, I effectively comprehended the lessons without having to understand the language spoken by the teacher because I was capable of visualizing and rationalizing the process taught. Since then, mathematics has been my favorite subject.

As a Data Analyst, I can pursue my lifelong dream of working in a field that interests me. By becoming a Data Analyst, I hope to provide knowledge that even those with a diverse language than English can successfully interpret. I hope to present work that will bring cognition to those who may not understand the data with well-defined and easy-to-understand visualizations, just as I was able to understand a class of mathematics taught in English with the same techniques.
